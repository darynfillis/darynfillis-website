About Daryn

Built to win the offer.
Built to last after it.

My work is helping buyers and homeowners make mortgage decisions that strengthen their position today while supporting their long-term financial future. Not just getting approved — knowing the strategy behind the approval.

As a Certified Mortgage Advisor, the focus is simple: structure the loan to help you win the offer, protect your cash flow, and keep future options open. That means planning before the transaction, disciplined execution during it, and guidance long after closing.

Before home financing, I spent 18 years in Mexico City leading businesses in fast-moving, consumer-driven environments — including serving as CEO and partner of a toy company. That background shapes how this work gets done: clear communication, strong systems, and decisions made intentionally rather than emotionally.
