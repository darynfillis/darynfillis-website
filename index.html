Our Process

A clearer path from concept to delivery.

We make the next step clear so your team can move from product concept to manufacturing, production, and delivery with better structure and less uncertainty.

Sampling and quality review Counter-sample development and production review help align quality, sizing, weight, and finishing before larger production moves forward.
01

Share the Product Need

Tell us what you want to make, including product description, quantity, target price, timeline, and reference materials.

02

Review Feasibility

The Atlas Global team reviews the opportunity, product requirements, and information needed to move forward.

03

Coordinate with Atlas Global LTD

Qualified opportunities are sent to Atlas Global LTD for sourcing review, pricing, production planning, and timeline guidance.

04

Review Quote + Sample Path

Once quote details are ready, we help present the opportunity clearly so your team can evaluate price, sample path, and timing.

05

Move Toward Production

After sample approval and final purchase terms, Atlas Global LTD coordinates production, logistics, and delivery according to written transaction terms.

Common Questions

Common sourcing questions.

What information do I need before requesting a quote?

Product description, reference sample or photos, tech pack if available, quantity, size range, fabric or composition, target price, timeline, packaging requirements, and shipping destination.

Can Atlas Global help source fabric from Asia?

Yes. Atlas Global can help review fabric sourcing opportunities, especially for companies producing in Mexico that need fabric or material support from Asia.

Who approves pricing and production timelines?

Atlas Global helps clarify pricing, timing, and next steps before the project moves forward.
